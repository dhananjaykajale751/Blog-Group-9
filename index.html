The need for ANI-CE:-

Every day we go outside we see there are number of stray animals like dogs, cats, cows, etc. These strays may be vaccinated or not we don’t have information about it. Such stray animals are very dangerous to the human beings. If a person comes in contact with such animal, it may lead to severe disease. Also, if a person comes in contact with an injured animal it may lead to infections, rashes and bacterial diseases. Along with this all those pains, all those sufferings to the strays we need to make a solution to all these problems. The rate of increase of stray dogs is at very high rate. The stray dogs are a big problem to the children in the area. While travelling on the road every time we see either a cat or a dog dead on the road due to accident. Such dead animals make the environment unclean. The smell caused by the decaying dead animals is extremely unbearable. The animals dead on the road are a big problem to bikers. Sometimes, such dead animals become the reason for big accidents. Such accidents may lead to several injuries or death of people. The animals dead due to any disease are very hazardous. Such dead animals can transmit their disease either to other animals or even to the human beings. There is a need to locate and dump these animals in such a way that it doesn’t affect the environment.

The functioning of ANI-CE:-

Our web application ANI-CE basically works for the wellbeing of the stray,abandoned and pet animals. Any nearby passer when sees any injured animal on the street can upload its photo or videos along with the location on the web application which further results in rescue of that animal by the near by NGO registered on the web application. ANI-CE also allows the registration of the NGOs and animal shelter homes to contribute to the mentioned cause

About

The ANI-CE is a project basically made for rescue and care of the stray animals. Also it will help to dump the dead animals which will help to keep the area clean and help to reduce the bacterial infections due to it. It will also help to find shelters homes for the pets of the pet parents.
